LSMN is the miniature variant of the LS life-sciences probe — same ±0.10 °C interchangeability and clinical-grade stability, with a sharply reduced form factor and a 0.4 s thermal time constant in stirred oil. Ideal for catheter-mounted sensors, fast-response patient monitoring, and microfluidic temperature loops where the standard LS probe head is mechanically too large.
