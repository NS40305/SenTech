The Custom Assembly programme is SEN TECH's full OEM sensor service: any of the eight NTC element families packaged into a customer-designed probe housing, with a customer-specified cable length, jacket material, and connector terminator. Typical builds: appliance NTC harnesses with Molex / JST connectors, automotive cabin probes with Teflon jackets, industrial weatherproof probes with stainless sheaths, and medical-grade reusable patient sensors. Send a mechanical drawing or a competitor part number — we ship qualification samples in 5 working days.
