The SM series is a surface-mount ceramic NTC built for harsh-environment, high-temperature applications where the standard chip-NTC tops out. The fired-ceramic body and metallised terminals withstand reflow profiles plus continuous operation up to +250 °C, making the SM series the SMT-friendly counterpart to the GB glass-bead and HAT axial-lead families. Typical use: industrial inverters, automotive engine compartment electronics, and commercial cooking appliances.
