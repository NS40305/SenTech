The CT series is a surface-mount chip NTC engineered for high-density PCB integration in consumer electronics, battery packs, and compact power circuits. Reflow-solderable, RoHS compliant, and available in standard EIA chip sizes for automated SMT assembly. The 0.9 mW/°C dissipation constant gives a tight self-heating margin while the 3–8 s thermal time constant supports fast monitoring of localized board hotspots.
