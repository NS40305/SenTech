GB series glass bead NTC thermistors hermetically seal the chip in fused glass for outstanding long-term stability and resistance to humidity, solvents, and aggressive media. Three sub-series (GBL / GBM / GBS) trade dissipation and time-constant for size, allowing the right balance of response speed and self-heating margin. Typical applications include low-cost probe assemblies, high-temperature printer ink sensing, PCB temperature sensing, air-conditioning systems, and high-temperature, high-speed tire manufacturing.
