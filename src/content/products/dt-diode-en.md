The DT series is a glass-sealed thermistor in a DO35 package — the same form factor familiar from rectifier diodes — combining mechanical robustness with a wide −40 °C to +250 °C operating range. UL1434 certification, RoHS exemption, and excellent thermal cycle endurance make it a workhorse for home appliance high-temperature monitoring, motor winding protection, and heater control where standard epoxy NTC parts run out of headroom. Various lead materials, diameters, and lengths available for OEM programs.
