The TR series is a stainless-steel encapsulated NTC thermistor probe designed for direct-immersion temperature sensing. The hermetically sealed metal sheath protects the chip from liquid, vapour, and chemical attack while delivering a 15 s in-water response. Typical use: water heaters, industrial process tanks, HVAC condenser sumps, and any application where the sensor body must survive direct media contact.
