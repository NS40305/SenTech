The IN series interchangeable NTC delivers tightly matched R/T curves so units can be swapped without recalibration. R25 options span 1, 2.52, 3, 5, 10, 20, 30, 50, and 100 kΩ; B25/85 values from 3435 K to 4262 K cover virtually any controller curve. Optimum stability is achieved within the specified temperature range; intermittent cycling between −50 °C and +150 °C is supported. Available with various lead materials, diameters, and lengths for OEM probe assemblies, thermostats, and field-replaceable temperature controllers.
