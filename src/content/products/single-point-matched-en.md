PM series point matched NTC thermistors are precision tested at a single chosen temperature point, delivering reliable accuracy at the working set-point without paying for full curve matching. Standard match points are 0 °C, 25 °C, 37 °C, 45 °C, 70 °C, and 100 °C. Optimum stability is achieved within the specified temperature range; intermittent cycling between −50 °C and +150 °C is supported. Custom match points, additional tolerance grades, and various lead options are available for HVAC, automotive electronics, and industrial equipment programs.
