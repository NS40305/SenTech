The LS series is a high-accuracy NTC probe designed for medical, clinical, and life-sciences instrumentation where ±0.10 °C interchangeability is the difference between a passing and failing diagnostic device. The probe houses a calibrated NTC element in a biocompatible-friendly housing suitable for reusable patient sensors, lab incubators, and bioreactor temperature loops. Custom probe lengths, lead materials, and connector terminations available for OEM medical-device programs.
